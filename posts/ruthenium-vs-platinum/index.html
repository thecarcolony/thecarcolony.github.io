Unveiling Ruthenium - The Next Level Spark Plug Material#

Ruthenium, a rare earth metal with atomic number 44 on the periodic table, has emerged as the next level spark plug material, surpassing both platinum and iridium. But what sets it apart from its counterparts? Let’s discover the two key factors that make Ruthenium HX spark plugs so remarkable - the Ruthenium alloy and the innovative spark plug tip design.

Unleashing the Power of the Ruthenium Alloy#

The Ruthenium alloy is the secret behind the unparalleled durability of these spark plugs. In the demanding conditions of modern engines, Ruthenium’s resistance to oxidation and peeling outperforms both iridium and platinum spark plugs. This means that NGK’s Ruthenium HX spark plugs offer the best service life for all engine types, ensuring optimal performance mile after mile.

High Ignitability Tip Designs: DFE and PSPE#

However, it’s not just the metal type that sets Ruthenium HX apart. NGK has taken it a step further by incorporating two advanced high ignitability tip designs - the Double Fine Electrode (DFE) and the Projected Square Platinum Electrode (PSPE). These cutting-edge designs are tailored for different engine types.

  • DFE (Double Fine Electrode): Designed for naturally aspirated engines, the DFE tip design maximizes ignitability and facilitates a more efficient combustion process. By reducing the absorption of the flame front inside the cylinder, this design delivers superior performance compared to conventional spark plugs.

  • PSPE (Projected Square Platinum Electrode): Turbocharged and supercharged applications require a specialized tip design, and here comes NGK’s PSPE tip design to the rescue. Its unique projection and square shape enhance ignitability and allow for optimal combustion, ensuring peak engine performance even under extreme conditions.

The Power of Complete Combustion#

Ruthenium HX spark plugs provide the most complete combustion event, enhancing engine efficiency and power output. With their advanced technology and exceptional durability, these spark plugs enable engines to operate at their peak performance over an extended period.

Embracing Advanced Technology in OE Replacements#

NGK understands the importance of offering high-quality replacement options to match the original equipment (OE) spark plug designs found in advanced engines. Ruthenium HX spark plugs bridge the gap between aftermarket demand and cutting-edge technology, providing an optimal solution for engine performance enhancement and longevity.

Conclusion#

In the realm of spark plug materials, Ruthenium has risen to the top, surpassing both platinum and iridium. NGK’s Ruthenium HX spark plugs combine the unmatched durability of the Ruthenium alloy with innovative high ignitability tip designs to provide engines with top-notch performance and longevity. Whether your engine is naturally aspirated or turbocharged, NGK has tailored a spark plug design to meet your specific needs.
