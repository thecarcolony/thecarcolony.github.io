Best Quiet Tire for Subaru in 2023 - Top 10 Quiet Tires Review

August 1, 2023 · 5 min · 894 words · Lisa Adams

When it comes to finding the best quiet tire for your Subaru, there are several factors to consider. Not only do you want a tire that reduces noise on the road, but you also want one that offers excellent traction and durability. In this article, we will review the top 10 quiet tires for Subaru in 2023, taking into account their features, prices, quality, durability, and customer feedback.

1. Yokohama Avid Touring S

The Yokohama Avid Touring S is our top pick for the quietest tire on the market. It features a symmetric tread design and silent shoulders, which significantly reduce noise in urban environments. The tire is also very responsive, thanks to the triplex tread compound. Traction, grip, and braking are excellent, making it suitable for more aggressive drivers. However, the snow traction is not as impressive as other tires on this list. The Avid Touring S comes with a 65,000-mile treadwear warranty.

2. Michelin Defender T Plus H

The Michelin Defender T Plus H is the most accomplished touring tire available but comes at a higher price point. It offers a supremely quiet ride on the highway and excellent ride quality. The tire is highly responsive and provides excellent high-speed stability. Traction, grip, and braking are impressive on dry, wet, and snowy roads. It also comes with an 80,000-mile treadwear warranty, the best in the touring category.

3. Cooper Discover HTP

The Cooper Discover HTP is an excellent budget-friendly option for a quiet highway tire. It provides a super quiet ride and plush ride quality on uneven roads. Performance-wise, traction, grip, and highway stability are top-notch. However, the tire’s snow traction is not as good as some others on this list. It comes with a 65,000-mile treadwear warranty.

4. Michelin Premier LTX

The Michelin Premier LTX tires are the dream come true for truck drivers, SUV owners, and light-duty van and crossover owners. These tires offer excellent traction on both wet and dry roads while providing a quiet and comfortable ride. The use of silica and sunflower oil in the tire’s tread compound enhances grip and braking performance. The new Premier LTX also features circumferential grooves that open up as the tires wear down, improving durability and tread life. Michelin offers a 60,000-mile treadwear warranty for these tires.

5. Michelin Defender All-Season Radial Tire

The Michelin Defender All-Season Radial Tire is perfect for any vehicle, including coupes, minivans, crossovers, and sedans. These tires provide excellent traction and a quiet ride, making them ideal for grand touring. They feature silica in their tread blocks, providing extra biting edges for wet traction. The tires are designed with two steel belts for strength and durability, and they come with a 90,000-mile warranty, one of the best in the industry.

6. Continental Cross Contact LX20

The Continental Cross Contact LX20 offers all-around performance for SUVs, crossovers, minivans, and pickups. The tires are designed for touring and all-season use, providing a comfortable and quiet ride. The tread design uses special polymers and additives to enhance traction and tread life, while the grooves help improve grip and reduce noise from airflow. Continental offers a 70,000-mile warranty for these tires.

7. Firestone Destination LE2

The Firestone Destination LE2 offers good traction on both dry and wet roads. The tires’ special groove pattern enhances traction, while sturdy shoulder blocks provide stability when navigating sharp bends. These tires excel in safety and offer a great ride experience. However, their snow traction may not perform as well as some other tires on this list.

8. Hankook Optimo All-Season Tire

The Hankook Optimo All-Season Tire offers a quiet and comfortable ride, effectively reducing road noise. With its tread depth of 13/32nd, it provides excellent handling in both rain and snow. The jointless cover makes the tire more durable, ensuring proper brake function. Hankook’s pitch design technology significantly reduces road noise. These tires are available for individual purchase or as a set of four matching tires.

9. Michelin Defender All-Season Radial Tire (Standard Touring)

The Michelin Defender All-Season Radial Tire in the standard touring category is designed for family cars, minivans, and crossovers. These tires provide a quiet ride while ensuring your loved ones’ safety. They offer excellent traction in a variety of conditions, including dry, wet, and wintry conditions. The tires feature a silica-based all-season tread compound and telesci technology, which minimizes rolling resistance, enhances wear rate, and provides a quiet and comfortable ride. Michelin offers a 90,000-mile warranty for these tires.

10. Milestar M-932 Sport All-Season Radial Tire

The Milestar M-932 Sport All-Season Radial Tire is known for its ability to reduce road noise and provide a quiet driving experience. These all-season tires offer universal appeal in various environments. They provide excellent traction without compromising noise levels. The thick and robust treads offer the right amount of grip for different road conditions. Milestar offers a warranty for these tires, ensuring customer satisfaction.

In conclusion, selecting the best quiet tire for your Subaru involves considering factors such as noise reduction, traction, durability, and price. The top 10 tires reviewed in this article offer a range of options for Subaru owners, allowing them to enjoy a quiet and comfortable ride. Choose the tire that best suits your needs and enjoy the benefits it brings to your driving experience.

Remember to always prioritize safety and consult with a tire professional before making your final decision.
